## Purpose

- Sender reputation and context evaluation

## Capabilities
1. **Content Analysis**: Parse and understand email content, attachments, and metadata
2. **Relevance Scoring**: Determine usefulness based on Barrot's goals and context
3. **Action Extraction**: Identify tasks, requests, and opportunities
4. **Knowledge Extraction**: Extract learnable information and insights
5. **Threat Detection**: Identify spam, phishing, and malicious content
6. **Context Integration**: Connect email content with existing knowledge base
7. **Priority Ranking**: Rank emails by potential value and urgency

## Intelligence Extraction
Barrot analyzes emails for:
- **Learning Opportunities**: Technical content, research, tutorials
- **Action Items**: Tasks, requests, deadlines
- **Opportunities**: Job offers, collaborations, partnerships
- **Intelligence**: Market trends, competitor info, industry insights
- **Social Context**: Relationship building, networking
- **Resource Links**: Useful tools, libraries, datasets, APIs

## Filtering & Categorization
- **High Value**: Actionable intelligence, opportunities, critical tasks
- **Medium Value**: Informational content, updates, newsletters
- **Low Value**: Routine notifications, automated messages
- **Noise**: Spam, promotional content, irrelevant messages
